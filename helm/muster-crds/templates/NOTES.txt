re-apply CRDs from crds/ — see UPGRADE.md for the schema-change ratchet.

`helm uninstall {{ .Release.Name }}` will NOT delete the CRDs (Helm does
not garbage-collect crds/). Remove them explicitly if and only if every
MCPServer / Workflow CR has been deleted first.
